BALTPRICE

Data provided "as is," no warranties

Description

House sales price and trend surface residuals for variogram analysis, Baltimore, MD 1978.

Type = dat file (input format required by Variowin), projected, X,Y on Maryland grid, projection type unknown.

Observations = 211

Variables = 6

Source

Original data made available by Robin Dubin, Weatherhead School of Management, Case Western Research University, Cleveland, OH, Robin.Dubin@weatherhead.cwru.edu. Trend surface residuals computed.

Reference: Dubin, Robin A. (1992). Spatial autocorrelation and neighborhood quality. Regional Science and Urban Economics 22(3), 433-452.
